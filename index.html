Our Services

Ultrasonic Testing

Uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws.

Visual Testing

Surface-level inspection for cracks, corrosion, and more.

PMI Testing

Verifies metal composition using XRF or OES technology.

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT)

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials using a dye or fluorescent penetrant.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT)

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials by applying magnetic fields and ferrous particles.

Ultra Sonic Thickness Gauging (UTG)

Ultrasonic Thickness Gauging (UTG) is a non-destructive testing method that uses high-frequency sound waves to measure the thickness of materials from one side, commonly used to assess corrosion or erosion.

Tensile Testing

Tensile Testing is a fundamental mechanical test used to determine how a material behaves under uniaxial tension.

DT Services

Destructive Testing (DT) is a testing method used to evaluate the mechanical and metallurgical properties of materials by subjecting them to failure under controlled conditions

Hardness Testing

Hardness Testing is a fundamental mechanical test used to determine a material’s resistance to indentation, penetration, or scratching

Bend Testing

Bend Testing is a mechanical destructive test used to evaluate the ductility, soundness, and quality of welds, base metals, and joints.

Proof Load Testing

Proof Load Testing is a mechanical testing method used to verify the strength and load-bearing capacity of equipment, lifting devices, structural components, and pressure-retaining items.

Charpy Impact Testing

Charpy Impact Testing is a destructive mechanical test used to determine the toughness or impact strength of materials..

AAS

Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) is an analytical technique used to determine the concentration of metals in a sample.

Spark Spectro

Spark Optical Emission Spectroscopy (Spark OES), commonly referred to as Spark Spectro, is a highly accurate method for determining the chemical composition of metals and alloys.

Rebound Hammer Test

The Rebound Hammer Test, also known as the Schmidt Hammer Test, is a non-destructive testing (NDT) method used to assess the surface hardness and estimated compressive strength of concrete.

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity Test

The Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity (UPV) Test is a non-destructive test (NDT) method used to evaluate the quality and uniformity of concrete.

Cover Meter Test

The Cover Meter Test is a non-destructive testing method used to determine the thickness of concrete cover over reinforcing steel (rebar) in RCC structures.

Half Cell Potential Testing

Half Cell Potential Testing is a non-destructive electrochemical method used to evaluate the likelihood of corrosion in steel reinforcement within reinforced concrete structures.

Carbonation Depth Measurement

Carbonation Depth Measurement is a concrete durability test used to determine the depth of carbonation in reinforced concrete structures.

Core Extraction & Compression Test

Core Extraction and Compression Testing is a destructive test used to determine the in-situ compressive strength of hardened concrete in structures.

Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT)

PAUT is an advanced NDT method using multiple ultrasonic elements and electronic time delays to create high-resolution images of internal structures.

Time-of-Flight Diffraction (TOFD)

TOFD is a highly accurate ultrasonic NDT technique used for detecting and sizing weld defects by measuring the diffraction of ultrasonic waves at defect tips.

Long Range Ultrasonic Testing (LRUT)

LRUT is an advanced ultrasonic testing method designed to inspect long lengths of welds, pipelines, and structural components from a single location using guided waves

Remote Visual Inspection (RVI)

Remote Visual Inspection (RVI), also known as Borescope or Videoscope inspection, is an advanced NDT method for inspecting internal areas of components that are otherwise inaccessible.

PQR/WPS Preparation

Procedure Qualification Record (PQR) and Welding Procedure Specification (WPS) preparation are critical steps in welding quality management. They ensure welding processes, materials, and techniques meet required standards.

Welding Inspection - WPQ Test

The Welder Performance Qualification (WPQ) test is conducted to qualify welders to perform welding according to specified codes, procedures, and joint designs. Our inspection ensures welds meet mechanical and visual requirements.

Failure Investigation

Failure investigation is a specialized service aimed at identifying the root cause of material or component failures. This process helps industries prevent recurrence, improve design, and maintain safety and reliability.

Hydrostatic Test

Hydrostatic testing is a method used to check the integrity and strength of pressure vessels, pipelines, and other fluid-containing components by filling them with liquid and applying pressure.

All Type Calibration

All Type Calibration services ensure that measuring instruments and equipment are accurate, reliable, and compliant with industry standards. Calibration helps maintain product quality, operational efficiency, and safety.
