NDT Inspection Services

Ultrasonic Testing

Uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws.

Visual Testing

Surface-level inspection for cracks, corrosion, and more.

PMI Testing

Verifies metal composition using XRF or OES technology.

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT)

Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT/PT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials using a dye or fluorescent penetrant.

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT)

Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT/MT) is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials by applying magnetic fields and ferrous particles.

Ultra Sonic Thickness Gauging (UTG)

Ultrasonic Thickness Gauging (UTG) is a non-destructive testing method that uses high-frequency sound waves to measure the thickness of materials from one side, commonly used to assess corrosion or erosion.

Radiography Testing (RT)

Radiography Testing (RT) is a non-destructive testing method that uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure of materials for hidden flaws or discontinuities.

Services (RTFI)

Radiographic Testing Film Interpretation (RTFI) is a specialized service in non-destructive testing (NDT) that involves the accurate evaluation of radiographic images (X-ray or Gamma-ray) to detect and assess internal discontinuities in welds, castings, and critical components

Eddy Current Testing (ET)

Eddy Current Testing (ET) is a non-destructive testing method that uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface defects in conductive materials.

RLA Studys

Residual Life Assessment (RLA) Studies are evaluations conducted on in-service equipment to estimate the remaining useful life by analyzing material degradation, operational history, and current condition through non-destructive and destructive testing methods.
